Extra info

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, until your website is fully deployed. Afterwards, you can only cancel without extra fees and no refund.

Are you willing to negotiate ways of payment?

Yes, within reason. We try to be flexible with clients.

What happens if I'm late to payments?

Again, we understand some clients may forget or have trouble paying. This is why we value communication in situations like these. If a client is a few days late, we will remind them. If a client is persistently late by months, we have the right to terminate our contract. This will include withdrawing your website.

How do I pay?

We use Stripe, where we send you a secure link via email to pay.

How long does it take to make a 5 page static website?

It depends on the website, your responsiveness and our speed, from between a few days to at most 3 weeks. This is why we ask for you to have information, images, designs, ideas, etc ready to send to us.
